The booking process for scheduling a team bonding event typically involves the following steps:
  1. Initial Consultation: We discuss your team's goals, preferences, budget, and logistics to understand your needs and customize the event accordingly.
  2. Proposal and Agreement: We provide a detailed proposal outlining the event details, pricing, terms and conditions, and any optional services or add-ons. Once the proposal is accepted, we proceed with signing an agreement to confirm the booking.
  3. Planning and Coordination: Our team works closely with you to finalize event logistics, coordinate venue arrangements, secure equipment and materials, and prepare for the event day.
  4. Event Execution: On the event day, our experienced facilitators and staff manage and facilitate the team bonding activities, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience for all participants.

Our cancellation policy for team bonding events is as follows:
  • Cancellation within 2 Months of the event: 50% of the total booking fee is refundable.
  • Cancellation within 1 Month of the event: 20% of the total booking fee is refundable.
  • Cancellation on the event day or no-show: No refund is provided.
  • We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise, and we strive to be flexible and accommodating while also ensuring fairness and sustainability for our business operations. Please refer to our terms and conditions or contact us for specific details regarding our cancellation policy.
